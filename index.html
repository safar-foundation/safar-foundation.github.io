About The Trust

Established for charitable and public purposes across India, we advance knowledge, equity, and sustainability through research, technology, and community empowerment.

A. Research & Policy

We conduct public interest research to shape equitable, evidence-based policies, studying technology’s impact and making research accessible.

B. Technology for Social Good

We develop open-source tools to address welfare and sustainability, promoting ethical tech adoption in underserved communities.

C. Education & Empowerment

We build digital capacity and critical awareness through accessible training, open-access tools, and gender equity programmes in tech.
